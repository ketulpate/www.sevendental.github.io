Meet Dr Gustavo Fischer

Principal Dentist

“My father was a dentist, and I remember being fascinated with the equipment and little bottles at the practice.” That fascination led Dr Gus, our Principal Dentist, to pursue a career in a field that allows him to change patients’ lives for good. An Interest in Biological Dentistry Dr Gus prioritises his holistic dental philosophy with a keen interest in biocompatible materials, minimally invasive treatment techniques, fluoride-free alternatives to caries management, safe mercury fillings replacement and all treatment approaches that biological dentistry has to offer. Please also remove the “any invasive or surgical procedure may carry risks. Before moving forward, it is recommended that you seek a second opinion from an appropriately licensed medical professional.

Education and Experience

Dr Gus earned his Bachelor of Dental Science with a Specialty in Periodontics from the University of Buenos Aires, Argentina in 1992. He also possesses an Australian Dental Council Certificate (BDS ADC). In addition, Dr Gus has more than 25 years of experience in dentistry and is a member of the International Academy of Oral Medicine & Toxicology (IAOMT). His experience also includes 12 years teaching Oral Implants and Periodontics in different universities and postgraduate training institutions. To stay abreast of the latest in dentistry.

Meet Dr Laura Curran

Associate Dentist

Dr Laura was inspired to become a dentist as she’s always liked working with people and wanted a job that was hands-on.

Education & Professional Development

To prepare for her career, Dr Laura earned a Bachelor of Oral Health and Graduate Diploma of Dentistry from Griffith University in 2012. Meet Dr Paola Cifuentes-Fischer

Principal Dentist

Principal Dentist Dr. PaolaDr Paola has always loved being able to help change how people feel about themselves by improving their oral health. Patients can display a confident smile knowing it not only looks good, but also functions well and promotes better overall health.

Family Inspiration

Dr Paola was inspired to make dentistry a career by watching her aunt in her practice. Her gentle, loving approach to her patients made a lasting impression on her. She prides herself on helping people whose past dental experience is based on very invasive procedures. With her conservative care and people-centred approach, patients feel comfortable knowing she’s working for them. A graduate of the University of Buenos Aires, Dr Paola earned her Bachelor of Dental Science in 1992. She is a member of the Australian Dental Association and continues her education in all areas of dentistry, with a focus on fixed prosthetics to provide her patients with the most up-to-date care.
